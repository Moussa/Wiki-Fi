About

Wiki-Fi is a tool that analyzes user activity on a wiki and produces graphs and metrics for display.

Currently supported wikis include the Official Team Fortress Wiki, the Portal Wiki, the Dota2 Wiki, and the Combine Overwiki.

Wiki-Fi makes use of the following software:

  • Python
  • MongoDB
  • Bootstrap
  • jQuery
  • Google Chart Tools
  • jQCloud
  • Heading Anchors
  • DataTables
View the source code on GitHub. Written by Moussekateer.

FAQ

  • Why is my username invalid?

    One of two reasons. The site only sees active users (users that have made at least one edit). The other reason is because it may not yet have come across one of your edits during its regular wiki activity updates. Wait a while.

  • Why is my edit count different from what the wiki says?

    The wiki never reduces edit counts when pages or files are deleted, while this site does. It also counts new file uploads as an edit.

  • How often are edit stats updated?

    Daily.
